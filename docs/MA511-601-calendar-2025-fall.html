MA 511: Advanced Calculus I — Fall 2025 Course Calendar
Week Date Range Lecture / Session Sections Covered Assignments
1 Aug 18 – Aug 22 Introduction to real numbers; logic and proof writing; sets and functions; bounded sets (supremum and infimum); Nested Interval Property. This week we cover the basics of logic and proofs and introduce sets of real numbers. The textbook in this course is not required; however, if you have it, note the section numbers for each lecture (we begin with a slightly different ordering of topics). Major concepts: Introduction to real numbers. Logic and proof writing. Sets and functions, bounded sets (supremum and infimum) and the Nested Interval Property. Introduce yourself on the class forum.
2 Aug 25 – Aug 29 Cardinality and density; basic topology of open and closed sets; the Cantor set and a quick look at dimension. Quiz 1
3 Sep 01 – Sep 05 Sequences: definition, convergence, boundedness; limit theorems for sequences.. Labor Day Quiz 2; Homework 1; Labor Day (no class on Monday).
4 Sep 08 – Sep 12 Cauchy sequences; subsequences; Monotone Subsequence Theorem; Monotone Convergence Theorem; Bolzano–Weierstrass Theorem. Quiz 3
5 Sep 15 – Sep 19 Continuity; consequences of continuity on closed intervals [a,b].. Wellness Day Quiz 4; Homework 2; Wellness Day (university holiday on Tuesday).; Midterm 1
6 Sep 22 – Sep 26 Partitions, lower and upper sums; integrability. Quiz 5
7 Sep 29 – Oct 03 Integrating discontinuous functions using measure; beginning of differentiability. Quiz 6; Homework 3
8 Oct 06 – Oct 10 Mean Value Theorem and preliminary results; Fundamental Theorem of Calculus; inverse functions. Quiz 7
9 Oct 13 – Oct 17 Review and assessment week for Midterm 2.. Fall Break (No classes) Quiz 8; Homework 4; Fall Break (no class Monday–Tuesday).; Midterm 2
10 Oct 20 – Oct 24 Sequences of functions and convergence. Quiz 9
11 Oct 27 – Oct 31 Limit theorems for sequences of functions; sup norm; metric spaces and examples. Quiz 10
12 Nov 03 – Nov 07 Contraction mapping; series of real constants. Quiz 11; Homework 5
13 Nov 10 – Nov 14 Weierstrass M-test for series of functions; power series and why they are useful. Quiz 12
14 Nov 17 – Nov 21 Taylor series (coefficients); an introduction to Fourier series. Quiz 13; Homework 6
15 Nov 24 – Nov 28 Wrap up, including a student choice activity.. Thanksgiving Holiday Thanksgiving Break (no class Wednesday–Friday).
16 Dec 01 – Dec 05 Review and wrap-up; LDOC; Midterm 3 week.. Reading Day; NC State Final Exam Period. See our scheduled dates in the Syllabus. Quiz 14; Homework 7; Student Choice Activity; Midterm 3
